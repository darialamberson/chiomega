Social

Like all girls, Chi Os love to have fun, and we have a lot of it. In joining Chi Omega, you not only get a group of sisters, but you also get a whole community. Through Chi Omega, you get to network with the whole Greek community, a sure way to expand your friend group beyond the friends you made in your dorm.

In addition, if you are a Chi Omega, your social calendar will always be filled. From Tour de Frats and Sigma de Chi O (our Cinco de Mayo celebration with Sigma Chi) to Special Dinners, Chi Os are always out having fun. Furthermore, if you want to go somewhere — be it a men's volleyball game, an all-campus party, or Coachella — you will always have a sister by your side.

So whether it is watching Glee together, making cards to send to our sisters abroad, going to a football game, dancing at Formal, winning Greek Week, or rolling out to a mixer with a fraternity, if we are together, you can be sure that we are having the time of our lives.
