Welcome!

Hello and welcome to our website! To all alumna and active members, welcome home. For those of you who are just visiting or are interested in recruitment, we encourage you to look around and get a feel for what being a Chi Omega is all about. Chi Omega is one of the most well-rounded groups of women on campus who constantly balance their academic, professional, philanthropic, and social activities. Being part of Chi Omega will give you a new home and a group of amazing friends to share your college experience with. Beyond that, the women of Chi Omega share a bond that truly lasts a lifetime.
